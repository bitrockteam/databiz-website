we are a group

From Databiz to Databiz Group

Born as an innovative system integrator in 2009, Databiz evolves in 2017 and transforms into a holding company (DatabizGroup), in order to scale fast, facing the evolving market scenario and aggregate innovative competences and methodologies addressing the Digital Transformation challenges. From this reorganization comes to light in 2018 Bitrock, a consulting and system integration company committed to offer avant-garde and innovative solutions in the field of Microservices, Reactive Platforms and DevOps, with the objective to provide reliable innovation through a completely different paradigm of projectuality, in order to understand the real needs of the clients and anticipate trends and evolutions. Databiz Group also includes Radicalbit: a software firm focused on the design and development of Fast Data and Agile Data Preparation products and solutions, offering enterprise solutions to data intense companies in order to empower competitive advantages.
